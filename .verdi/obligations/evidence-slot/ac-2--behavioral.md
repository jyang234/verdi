# An empty slot badges, opens its receipt, and blocks nothing

The behavioral evidence must show an empty declared kind producing a badge
whose derivation record names the derived-tree location probed and states
per-kind that nothing was found, with digest revisions and no timestamp;
and on the same badged authoring wall must exercise at least one write
path (add a sticky, or draw and commit yarn) and prove it succeeds —
the empty slot disclosed the hole and refused nothing (co-2).
